  • Yuva Kranti Nandurbar is a youth-driven Adivasi organization committed to the empowerment, awareness, and upliftment of Adivasi society in the Nandurbar district. Our movement is rooted in the values of Jal, Jungle, Zameen and the rich traditions, language, culture, and identity of Adivasi communities. We believe that educated, aware, and united youth are the true strength of social change. Through awareness campaigns, social activism, cultural programs, and support for justice and rights, Yuva Kranti works to raise voices against injustice and stand firmly for equality, dignity, and constitutional rights. Our mission is to inspire Adivasi youth to become socially responsible, confident leaders who protect their heritage while shaping a better future. We strive to create a strong platform where youth can come together, learn, organize, and work for the development of society.
